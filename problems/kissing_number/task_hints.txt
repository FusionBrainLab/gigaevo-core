OPTIMIZATION OBJECTIVE

Fitness Metric
Maximize N = number of distinct non-zero integer vectors x_i ∈ Z^12 satisfying:
    ||x_i||^2 = r^2 (constant) and min_{i≠j} ||x_i − x_j||^2 ≥ r^2
(After rescaling by t = 2/sqrt(r^2), all centers lie on radius 2 and pairwise distances ≥ 2.)

Failure Modes
- Norm inconsistency (not single-shell)
- Certificate violation: d2_min < r^2
- Duplicate vectors or inclusion of zero vector
- Symmetry duplicates (sign/permutation copies)
- Non-integer coordinates
- Float-based checks instead of exact integers

Evaluation Details
- r^2 = ||x_i||^2 (common squared norm)
- d2_min = min_{i≠j} ||x_i − x_j||^2
- Valid iff d2_min ≥ r^2
- Fitness = N; secondary metric: margin δ = d2_min − r^2
- Target: N ≥ 840
